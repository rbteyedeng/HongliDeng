Some Projects

Microsoft

Spatial Presenter for Microsoft Team

I lead the development of an AR system built for Microsoft Teams which extracts person from video streams and integrates them into a 3D virtual stage using RGB webcam only. It uses 3D understanding, person segmentation and graphics rendering to deliver authentic and immersive virtual presence for the users.


Demo to be coming soon...


Microsoft Dynamic 365 Connected Store

I lead the development of DCS (Microsoft Dynamic Connected Store) SaaS system for retail customers. It is a spatial analysis system that do scene understanding, object detection and tracking, video event inferencing to extract business insights such as customer traffic, merchandise engegement time, queue waiting time etc.



Person Body Orientation Understanding

I designed the 3D human body orientation detection from RGB cameras to get the endcap engagement time for retail customers.



Amazon

Women Dress Classification

I trained 5 dress models (Hemline, neck style, pattern, silhouette and sleeve type) using deep neural network. They are deployed to Amazon.com. Search “women dresses” at Amazon and look for neckline, pattern, sleeve type, silhouette and hemline on the left pane. https://www.amazon.com/s?k=women+dress&ref=nb_sb_noss_2



Amazon: FashionDNA

I initialized and led the development of the internal tool for Amazon Fashion group which realized superfast customized model training from scratch via transfer learning. It enabled fashion specialist team build 21 models in a single day for the Women’s shoes category with an average accuracy of 90%, using 2K images per model. It makes it feasible for Amazon US to tag its 500 million products in a short timeline.



Amazon: Discover your style
I provided the techniques and helped building this system for Amazon Hardline group. It achieved image-based interactive customer shopping (https://www.amazon.com/discover/).

Inspired by Shopping

A demo system of Amazon fashion image search. Using other fashion retailers’ online lists to search Amazon’s catalog to provide products with similar looking and attributes.
